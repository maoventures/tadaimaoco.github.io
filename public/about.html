Tadaimao & Co.

Tadaimao & Co. (located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia) is the parent company to several different ventures with a unified focus on empowering creators, makers and entrepreneurs alike by integrating empathy with technology, seamlessly.

From creating process-powered solutions for digital adoption and tech integration, to offering customised services by leveraging on the power of social capital - we empower our communities through knowledge sharing, peer support and advocating for collaborative solutions to the systemic issues within the startup landscape.

Learn more about us below.

Palindrome Labs

Palindrome Labs is the R&D venture under Tadaimao & Co. focused on investigating the process of content creation and the concept of social capital and influence in the digital sphere using new platforms and mediums, per the new normal post-pandemic.

Dedicated to developing a process-oriented progressive solutions, and human-focused digital workflows - #teamtadaimao advocates for efficient productivity, the use of social capital to power content marketing strategies and efforts in reducing creator burnout.

Presently developing the web app for the Palindrome Framework - a component-based, modular visual tool with a methodology based on the core tenets of lean, agile and Six Sigma - #teamtadaimao asks: why not treat content creation like product  or software development? With six templates for various use cases, follow their progress online and see how Palindrome forms the core of our upcoming SaaS product, ContentOS: a single CMS that integrates the digital tools creators use daily into a single source for content creation, creating a new way to create.

Humanity Inc Asia

Humanity Inc. Asia is a management consultancy focused on tech advisory and enablement for startups. Realising the knowledge and digital skillset gap in startups and other SMEs after a number of local startups in the region failed to make that digital pivot during the first three months of the pandemic in 2020, Humanity Inc. Asia made the decision to nurture digital adoption and accelerate efforts in creating a true digital hub in Malaysia for the ASEAN region.

With that in mind, the consultancy cultivated a global network of like-minded independent consultants, over 40 SaaS product, and company partnerships and affiliates, constantly upskilling #teamtadaimao to keep up with global trends and develop digital strategies, solutions and services tailored towards the underserved startup landscape, whether here or on a global scale.

That said, our speciality is building custom software suites(tech stacks) for startups, allowing them to focus on their original purpose. These tech stacks showcase both availability of alternative products to typical ERPs, such as Salesforce, Oracle and SAP; as well as educating them on the advancements in technology today - such as GPT-3 for marketing copy, the ease of use for low-code/no-code products as well as the endless possibilities for new solutions based on current platforms and best practices.

korefortynine studios

korefortynine studios (stylised as kore49), is the brand name for founder Hannah Azlan’s consulting services in content creation, brand development and digital marketing - with a focus on authentic, aligned branding and social media strategies. That said, the digital polymath has broadened both team size and service offerings to include web development and design, photography and videography as well as social media management by incorporating #teamtadaimao into the evolution of the brand.

With an emphasis on quality over quantity, korefortynine only takes on a limited amount of clients at any given point, choosing to work on projects that spark joy and are in alignment with both team and founder alike. For more enquiries, contact her directly via the links in her Instagram.
